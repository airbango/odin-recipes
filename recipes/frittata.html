Frittata

Description

Ingredients

  • 6 eggs
  • 10oz spinach (fresh or frozen)
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 tbsp butter or cooking oil
  • 1/2 tbsp of black pepper
  • A dash of salt
  • Spices and/or herbs to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
  2. Chop fresh spinach or thaw and drain frozen spinach.
  3. In a mixing bowl, whisk six eggs.
  4. Add spinach and shredded cheese to the mixing bowl and combine.
  5. Add salt, pepper, and herbs/spices to taste.
  6. Coat a 9 inch pie dish with butter or cooking oil.
  7. Pour contents of mixing bowl into pie dish.
  8. Bake in heated oven for 50 minutes, or until top begins to brown slightly.
  9. Cut and serve with a pie server, and enjoy!
