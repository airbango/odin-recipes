Mulled Cider

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 gallon apple cider
  • 1 orange
  • 2/3 cups dried cranberries
  • 1 whole nutmeg seed
  • 2 whole cinnamon sticks
  • 2 allspice berries
  • 2 whole cloves

Steps

  1. Begin heating a large pot to medium-high heat.
  2. Crack the nutmeg seed into 2-4 pieces with a nutcracker or hammer and cloth.
  3. Add nutmeg pieces, cinnamon sticks, allspice berries, and cloves to the pot.
  4. Toast spices until fragrant.
  5. Add cider to pot and continue heating.
  6. Slice orange into thin rounds and add to pot.
  7. Add dried cranberries to pot.
  8. Once the cider is warm, turn off heat or set to low and serve.
  9. (Optional) Pour cider through a strainer to avoid whole spices in your glass.
