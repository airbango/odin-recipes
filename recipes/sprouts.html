Roasted Brussels Sprouts

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 pound fresh brussels sprouts
  • 1/4 cup parmasean cheese
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp granulated garlic
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
  2. Cut the brown ends off the brussels sprouts.
  3. Halve brussels sprouts along the ends you just cut.
  4. In a large mixing bowl, combine brussels sprouts, oil, and spices.
  5. Toss brussels sprouts until evenly coated.
  6. Cover a baking sheet with one sheet of aluminum foil.
  7. Place the brussels sprouts flat side down on the foil.
  8. Bake for 35-40 minutes until browned.
  9. After removing from the oven, sprinkle parmasean cheese, then serve.
